Tender type

 

Use this screen to choose a tender type for pickup.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Enter the tender amount, then choose an option from the navigation bar on the right side of the screen:

 

 

Local Cash

Choose this option if the customer is paying with local cash.

 

Canadian Cash

Choose this option if the customer is paying with Canadian cash.

 

Local Checks

Choose this option if the customer is paying with a local check.

 

Canadian Checks

Choose this option if the customer is paying with a Canadian check.

 

 

Press Undo to return to return to the previous screen.

 

 Press Cancel to exit the function.
