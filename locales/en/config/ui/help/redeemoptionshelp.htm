Redeem Options

 

Use this screen to choose a refund tender when redeeming a gift card, gift certificate or merchandise certificate.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Choose a refund tender option from the navigation bar on the right side of the screen:

 

Gift Card

Choose this option to apply the refund to a gift card.

 

Gift Cert.

Choose this option to apply the refund to a gift certificate.

 

Store Credit

Choose this option to apply the refund to store credit.

 

 

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.
