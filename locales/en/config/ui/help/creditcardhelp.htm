Credit Tender

To tender a transaction using a credit card, do this:

·         On the Tender Options screen, the balance due is displayed.  If the balance due is not equal to the amount of the credit tender, type the amount of the credit tender and press F4/Credit. 

·         When the Credit Card screen appears, swipe the card or type the credit card number (minimum 10 digits) and expiration date (mmyyyy), and press Enter/Next.

·         You return to the Tender Options screen to complete the tender if the credit tender is less than the balance due.

·         When the credit tender is completed, you return to the Sell Item screen if the credit tender is for the entire amount due, and the credit authorization process begins automatically.
