Inquiry Options

Item Inquiry

·         To do an item inquiry, press F2/Item.  The Item Search screen appears.

Gift Card Inquiry

To check the balance or expiration date of a gift card, perform these steps:

·         Press F3/Gift Card.  The Gift Card screen appears.

 

Gift Receipt Inquiry

To check the price of an item returned with a gift receipt, perform these steps:

·         Press F4/Gift Receipt.  The GR Inquiry screen appears.
