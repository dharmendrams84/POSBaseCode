Debit Tender

To tender a transaction using a debit card, do this:

·         From the Tender Options screen, press F5/Debit.

·         When the Debit Card screen appears, swipe the card.  The PIN Entry screen appears.

·         On the PIN Entry screen you are prompted to ask the customer to enter their PIN on the PIN Pad.  Tell the customer to type their PIN and press Enter/Next.

Note:   The PIN must have between 4 and 12 characters.

·         You return to the Tender Options screen and a debit tender line is added to the transaction.  If the amount accounts for the entire balance of the purchase, the debit authorization process begins automatically.  Proceed to the following step.
