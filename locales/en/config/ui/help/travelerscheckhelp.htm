Traveler’s Check

 

Use this screen to enter the total number of traveler’s checks the customer has presented as payment. In a later screen, you are prompted to enter the value of each individual traveler’s check. All traveler’s checks must be franked.

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Enter the total number of traveler’s checks the customer has presented, then press Next.

 

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.

 

Press Cancel to exit the function.

 

Press Delete to clear the current field entry.

 

Guidelines For Accepting Traveler’s Checks

 

Remember the following guidelines when accepting traveler’s checks as tender:

 

Make sure that the traveler’s checks are in US currency. Traveler’s checks in foreign currencies are not accepted.

 

Have the customer sign the traveler’s check in your presence. Make sure the counter signature matches the original signature.

 

The system will automatically calculate the remaining balance due.

 

Valid traveler’s check amounts are $20, $25, $50, $75, $100, $200, $500 and $1000.

 

All traveler’s checks must be franked.
