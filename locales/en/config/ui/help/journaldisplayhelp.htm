Journal Display

 

This screen displays a journal entry that meets the search criteria you previously entered. Only one journal entry is displayed at a time. Use the navigation buttons on the right side of the screen to advance between entries. You may not view the entire journal for all transactions in your store at one time.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

View the information in the journal entry to determine if it contains the information you are looking for. To locate information, choose an option from the navigation bar on the right side of the screen:

 

Previous

Choose this option to navigate to the previous journal entry of your search results.

 

Note: You may not choose Previous on the first journal entry of your search results.

 

Next

Choose this option to navigate to the next journal entry of your search results.

 

Note: You may not choose Next on the last journal entry of your search results.

 

First

Choose this option to navigate to the first journal entry of your search results.

 

Last

 

Choose this option to navigate to the last journal entry of your search results.

 

Done

 

Choose this option when you are finished viewing the electronic journal.

 

Print

 

Choose this option to print the journal entries returned in your search results. 

 

Note: An indicator prints on the receipt to identify the receipt as a journal entry so that it cannot be used as a customer receipt.

 

Note: You may not be able to see all of the transaction items on the screen at once. Use the scroll bar to view more items on the journal entry.

 

 

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.

 

Press Cancel to exit the function.
