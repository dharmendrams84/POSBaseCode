Temporary Employee Master

 

Use this screen to enter or modify information for a temporary employee, reset the temporary employee’s password, or capture the temporary employee's fingerprint.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Enter the employee information, then press Next.

Note: Pressing Next saves the information on this screen to the employee database.

 

Choose an option from the navigation bar on the right side of the screen:

 

Reset Password

Choose this option to reset the temporary employee’s password.

 

Set Fingerprint

Choose this option to capture the temporary employee’s fingerprint.

 

 

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.

 

Press Cancel to exit the function.
