Scan or Enter Check

 

Use this screen to scan the check through the MICR reader or manually enter the information when accepting a check as tender.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Place the check in the MICR reader for scanning or manually enter the information (the bank account and routing number on the bottom of the check).

                                                                     

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.

 

Press Cancel to stop the tendering function and return to the Sell Item screen.

 

Press Delete to clear the current field entry.
