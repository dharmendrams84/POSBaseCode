Device Status

 

This screen reports on the status of all devices attached to the base unit of the register. Online displays in green next to a device to indicate that it is properly configured and may be used. Offline displays in red next to a device to indicate that it is improperly configured or the system is not receiving a response from the device.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Press Next to return to the Manager Options screen.

 

 

Tips For Troubleshooting Devices

 

If a device is offline, attempt the following:

 

Make sure the device is powered on.

 

Reset (unplug and re-plug) the connections to the base unit and customer terminal to make sure they are tightly in place.

 

Restart the register (turn it off, wait five seconds, then turn it back on).

 

Call Store Support at 1-800-241-2626 for additional help if the above steps do not resolve the offline status.
