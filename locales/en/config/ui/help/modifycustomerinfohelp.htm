Change Customer Information

To change information for an existing customer, perform these steps:

·         Find the customer as described in Find Customer.

·         The Customer Contact screen appears.  Make any desired changes on the Customer Contact screen.

·         If you want to link the customer to the current transaction, press F2/Link.

·         If you are finished changing the customer information and you do not want to save the changes, press F3/Done.

Link Customer

To link an existing customer to a transaction, perform one of these steps:

·         If you know the customer number, you can link them from the Customer Options screen.  At the prompt, type the customer ID and press Enter/Next.

·         Find a customer (Find Customer), press Enter/Next, and press F2/Link.  You return to the Sell Item screen.

·         Add a customer (Add Customer) and press F2/Link.  You return to the Sell Item screen.
