ORPOS Main Options

 

Use this screen to select a POS option.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

Choose an option from the navigation bar on the right side of the screen:

 

Daily Ops

Choose this option to maintain daily operations.

 

POS

Choose this option to enter the Point-of-Service system.

 

Administration

 

Choose this option to maintain administration functions.

Clock In/Out

 

Choose this option to clock in or out.

Fulfillment

 

Choose this option to maintain the fulfillment functions.

Browser

 

Choose this option to open a web browser for access to a specific website.

 

Training On

 

Choose this option to begin the training mode session.

Inventory Inquiry

 

Choose this option to request inventory information on an item.
