Add Customer

To add a customer to the database, perform these steps:

·         From the Customer Options screen, press F3/Add to access the Add Customer screen.

·         Type the requested information.  The minimum information required to add a customer is indicated by red asterisks beside the field names.

·         When you have entered the information, press Enter/Next. 

·         When the Customer Master screen appears, make any additional changes necessary, and press Enter/Next.

·         When the Customer Contact screen appears, make any additional changes and press F2/Link to link the customer to an open transaction. 

If you are finished adding the customer and you do not want to link to a transaction, press F3/Done to commit the information to the database.
