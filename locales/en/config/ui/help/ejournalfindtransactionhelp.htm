Electronic Journal

 

The Electronic Journal tracks transaction details for registers. Use this screen to view entries in the Electronic Journal.

 

·       If the Electronic Journal is being written to a file, only information for your register can be displayed.

·       If the Electronic Journal is being written to the database, you can access information for another register from your register.

 

What Can I Do On This Screen?

 

On the Admin. Options screen, press F2/E. Journal. The Find Transaction screen is displayed:

 

·       To search by Transaction ID, enter the range of Transaction IDs and press Enter.

·       To search by any of the other criteria, enter the date, time, register number, user ID, or Sales Associate ID. Press Enter.

 

Note: If you do not enter any search criteria, the system searches for all transactions for the current business day.

 

 

Press Undo to return to the previous screen.

 

Press Cancel to exit the function.
