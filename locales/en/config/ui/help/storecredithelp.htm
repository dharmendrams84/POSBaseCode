Store Credit

When a customer return or exchange results in a negative balance due, you can issue a store credit, which can be redeemed anytime before the expiration date toward the purchase of other merchandise.

 

Store credit is validated using three pieces of information: the store credit amount, the store credit number, and the expiration date.

Redeem Store Credit

From the Tender Options screen, a store credit can be used to tender a transaction.  To tender a Store Credit, perform these steps:

·         On the Tender Options screen, press F9/More and press F3/Store Credit.  The Store Credit screen appears.

·         On the Store Credit screen, scan the store credit or type the number and press Enter/Next.  Continue with Store Credit Found or Store Credit Not Found, as appropriate.
