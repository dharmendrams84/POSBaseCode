Store Credit

When a customer return or exchange results in a negative balance due, you can issue a store credit, which can be redeemed anytime before the expiration date toward the purchase of other merchandise.

 

Store credit is validated using three pieces of information: the store credit amount, the store credit number, and the expiration date.

Store Credit Not Found

·         If the Store Credit is not found, the Store Credit Info screen appears.  Type the amount of the certificate and the expiration date, and press Enter/Next.

·         The store credit is deducted from the purchase and:

–        If the balance due is zero, the tender is completed.

–        If the balance due is a positive amount, you are prompted to select an additional tender option for the remaining balance.

–        If the balance is a negative amount, an additional store credit is issued for the difference, and a store credit receipt prints.  Provide the store credit to the customer and tell them they will need this receipt to redeem the remainder of their store credit.

·         The Store Credit Amount screen appears. 

·         Verify that the amount you typed is correct. 

–        If the amount is incorrect, press No and retype the amount. 

–        If it is correct, press Yes to verify the amount and return to the Tender Options screen.

·         The Tender Options screen appears, indicating the store credit has been accepted.

·         The Tender Options screen displays either:

–        The additional balance due for other tender type, or

The over tender amount (if over tender is allowed), and you are prompted to provide the customer the correct amount of change.
